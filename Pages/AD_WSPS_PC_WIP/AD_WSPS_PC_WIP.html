Mechanism

  1. Shocks to aggregate demand (demand-side) or the labour market (supply-side) are permanent and occur in the initial period 0.
  2. If the short-run AD does not provide employment consistent with the long-run labour market equilibrium, a bargaining gap appears: \(\bf{\text{BG}=\cfrac{w^{WS} - w^{PS}}{w^{PS}}}\).
  3. Each period, the bargaining gap is added to that period's realised inflation, and next period's expected inflation.
